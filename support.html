Little Brain Learners

Last Updated: May 2025

Little Brain Learners (the "App") is designed with a commitment to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and how we comply with the Google Play Families Policy and COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

1. Data Collection and Usage

Little Brain Learners does not collect, transmit, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) with the developer or any third parties. All user-provided data is stored locally on your device.

Types of Data Stored Locally:

  • Player Profiles: Names and ages (used to customize game difficulty and track individual progress).
  • Photos: Images selected for the "Family Match" game mode (used only for in-game avatars).
  • Emergency Contact Numbers: Phone numbers provided in "Safety Practice" mode.
  • Game Statistics: High scores, accuracy, and session duration.

Note: None of this data ever leaves your device. We do not have servers, and we do not use cloud storage for user data.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) to track your usage.
  • Advertising: Little Brain Learners is ad-free. No advertising IDs are collected.
  • In-App Purchases: We use standard Google Play and Apple App Store billing systems. We do not see or store your credit card or financial information.

3. Children's Privacy

Little Brain Learners is specifically designed for children.

  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not use social media plugins.
  • We do not use any external tracking.

4. Parental Gate

To ensure children do not accidentally access external content or make purchases, a Parental Gate (e.g., a math challenge or specialized gesture) is required for:

  • Accessing the "About" screen or support links.
  • Accessing the "Safety Practice" settings.
  • Triggering In-App Purchase flows.

5. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all information associated with the app at any time by:

  1. Clearing the App Data/Cache in your device settings.
  2. Uninstalling the App.
