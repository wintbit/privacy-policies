Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Ernel Wint ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BrainMatch mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our app, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Player Name
  • Player Age
  • Photos (for family member profiles)

2. Use of Data

BrainMatch uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our app (e.g., Family Mode)
  • To provide game statistics and progress tracking

3. Camera and Photo Storage

Our app requires access to the camera to allow you to create personalized profiles for family members. These photos are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, share, or transmit these photos to any external servers.

4. Data Storage

All player data, including names, ages, and game statistics, is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a central database of your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for use by families, including children. We do not collect personally identifiable information from children without parental consent. Any data entered (like names or ages) is stored only on the device and is used solely for gameplay personalization.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us
